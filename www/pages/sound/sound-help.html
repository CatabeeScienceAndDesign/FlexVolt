Sound Help

The FlexVolt app can generate auditory feedback based on your muscles signals. This can be very useful if you want to monitor your muscle contraction levels without watching them on a screen!

There are two audio feedback modes:

  • Proportional - the pitch (frequency) of the tone changes with changes in muscle signal intensity. Higher pitch means stronger signals.
  • 2. Threshold - hear a tone when your muscle signal passes a set level
    • * Above Threshold - set a target level in microvolts, uV, and hear a tone when your muscle contraction signal is above that level.
    • * Below Threshold - set a target level in microvolts, uV, and hear a tone when your muscle contraction signal is below that level.
