Myometer Help

Feedback

  • Reach a minimum level of exersion by bringing the signal level above the threshold
  • Practice muscle relaxation by keeping the exersion level below the threshold

Thresholds

  • Set thresholds by dragging the gray bars up and down.

Baselines

  • With muscle in relaxes position, click 'set' button
  • Wait for measurement to complete
  • Channel is now adjusted with baseline as 0

Name Channels

  • Click a channel name to change it
